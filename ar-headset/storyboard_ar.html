Storyboard A — Evening Routine

1
Living Room · Evening

Robert arrives home and lifts the AR headset. He puts it on — the lenses click into place.

2
System Boots

INTELLIDEN loads instantly. The Hub overlays the room — temperature, active scene, and node count visible at a glance.

3
Alert Detected

An orange alert pulses in the HUD. The entrance door is open. Robert gazes toward the alert card.

4
Rooms Screen

He gazes at the Rooms tab and pinches. Six large room tiles appear — each showing status and brightness at a glance.

5
Gaze + Pinch · Toggle

Gaze locks on Living Room. One pinch — lights on at 75%. A green checkmark confirms the action instantly.

6
Done · No Phone Needed

Task complete in under 10 seconds. No phone, no app. Robert sets the headset down — home is already set up for the evening.
