Storyboard — Morning Alert

Intelliden · Smartwatch · Alex fixes a power issue from the wrist

1
Wrist glance
kitchen · morning

Alex is making coffee. Raises wrist — watch face shows the time and live power draw bar at the bottom.

2
Alert vibrates
haptic vibration

Watch vibrates on the wrist. A red alert appears — "Overload · 3.2kW." Two buttons: Fix or Dismiss.

3
Tap "Fix"
one tap

Alex taps "Fix." The Analytics screen opens directly — showing today's energy and a sparkline chart.

4
Stats screen
analytics screen

Screen shows 48.7 kWh used, room breakdown bars, and an HVAC warning — using 18% more than normal.

5
Problem fixed
confirmed ✓

Green checkmark on watch. Screen shows "HVAC optimised — saving 28 RON." One tap, done in seconds.

6
Walks out, done
no phone needed

Alex walks out the door. Watch face is back to normal — power draw is lower. Whole task done without touching the phone.
